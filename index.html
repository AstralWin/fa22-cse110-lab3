Recap on previous meeting

Finalized the appointment of the new Board of Directors. Now former president, Jay Johnson, prepared a farewell speech for the entire organization. The following is a brief part of his speech: "To all my fellow club members, it has been a wonderful time serving as your president over the last 3 years. My journey has come to a close, but we will meet again."

The new board of directors, led by incoming president Alwin Low laid out some expectations and rules to be followed.
i. No drinking
ii. Always be respectful.
iii. Always have fun

Finalized the organization of the social party. Started ironing out details such as the budget and timeslot. Nelson Sim was chosen to lead the project and was tasked with inspecting the venue. Full report on that expected today.

Note: It was an emotional day for everyone involved.
